Asian Flavors

Mongolian Sauce:

This Mongolian Sauce recipe offers a perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors to complement the Mongolian Pork dish featured below. Begin by heating olive oil in a pan before whisking together ground ginger, minced garlic, lite soy sauce, dark brown sugar, water, and cornstarch in a bowl. Add the mixture to the skillet and heat, allowing the flavors to meld together beautifully. As the sauce simmers, whisk in cornstarch or flour to achieve a luscious, thick consistency. After simmering for just 5 minutes, remove from heat and serve this delectable sauce over your favorite meat dishes for a culinary delight that's sure to impress even the most discerning palates.

Mongolian Pork:

This Mongolian Beef/Pork recipe promises tender, flavorful meat bathed in a savory sauce, perfect for satisfying your Asian cuisine cravings. Start by marinating thinly sliced beef in a mixture of water, low sodium soy sauce, cornstarch, white pepper, garlic powder, and baking soda for maximum tenderness. Then, heat sesame oil in a wok or large skillet until just below smoking point and sear the marinated meat in batches until crispy. Remove the meat and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant before adding the beef back to the pan. Drizzle with Mongolian sauce, allowing it to thicken slightly and coat the meat evenly. Garnish with sesame seeds for added crunch and serve piping hot for a delightful dining experience that's sure to impress.

Fried Rice:

This Jasmine Fried Rice recipe promises to elevate your dining experience with its aromatic flavors and satisfying textures. Begin by preparing 1.5 cups of Jasmine rice in a rice cooker and spreading it thinly on a baking sheet, allowing it to cool and firm up in the refrigerator for two hours. Once chilled, heat a wok over high heat and melt butter before adding minced garlic for a fragrant base. Next, add the prepared rice and stir-fry until each grain is coated in buttery goodness. Finish by incorporating soy sauce and Kikkoman Fried Rice Seasoning, ensuring a harmonious blend of savory and umami flavors throughout. With just a few simple steps, this delectable dish is ready to serve, promising to delight your taste buds with each flavorful bite.
